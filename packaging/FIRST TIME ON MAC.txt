PhotoSorter — first time on Mac
================================

After you download and extract the files, put them in your photos folder.
You should see:

  • Open PhotoSorter.command   ← use this to start the app
  • PhotoSorter.app

FIRST TIME ONLY (macOS blocks apps from the internet):

  1. Right-click "Open PhotoSorter.command"
  2. Choose "Open"
  3. Click "Open" in the dialog

Every time after that, just double-click "Open PhotoSorter.command".

If macOS still blocks it, open System Settings → Privacy & Security
and click "Open Anyway" (shown after a blocked launch attempt).
